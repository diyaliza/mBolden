About Us

Meet Suzanne...

CEO of mBolden and DMZ’s 2023 Woman of the Year, Suzanne Knight is a trusted advisor for executives and inspirational leader for teams.

She has led 40+ transformations in 12 countries as an executive at mBolden, Walmart Canada, Philip Morris International, and Deloitte.

As a respected leader in her field, she operates as a global keynote speaker, covering the future of work, leading change, embedding innovation into ways of thinking and working, and building an agile organization and mindset.

Suzanne holds an MA in Industrial/Organizational Psychology and an MBA in Finance and Strategy.

01.

Strategic Planning

We start with corporate vision, objectives, and values and create a structured plan to align operations, technology, culture, data, talent, and other areas depending on objectives. Focus could prioritize digital enablement, ways of thinking, ways of working, or culture change.

02.

Organization Design & Alignment

Organizations are systems that include strategy, governance, metrics, policies, processes, structures, talent, data, technology, and change. Aligning each component can enhance productivity, efficiency, and customer and employee experience.

03.

Leadership & Team Development

Learning and coaching to empower leaders and their teams with the skills and mindset to perform in a dynamic and fast-paced world. Each must balance 'business as usual' while managing ongoing change and preparing for the future. We have a series of keynotes and workshops on topics including operating without silos and managing change, to upskill and engage the driving force of your organization.

04.

Workforce Planning

How organizations work has evolved with globalization, technology, adaptation to remote or hybrid work, and generational expectations. We optimize structures and workforce composition, as well as where and how people work, to adapt to changing landscapes. Our approach is to identify customer and business priorities and identify, then implement, the most efficient systems of work, while integrating cutting-edge technologies

05.

Diversity, Equity, Inclusion & Belonging

Teams will not reach full potential until each member feels valued, respected, and has equal opportunities for success. Be the inclusive employer of choice for top talent. We can conduct diagnostics, build a strategy and plan, align leaders, and take action. Attract top talent, engage the workforce, drive innovation, and maximize performance.

06.

Change, Communications & Training

Navigate organizational and external change by ensuring awareness, understanding, buy-in, and ideally championship. Build a shared sense of urgency, respect contributions toward success of the initiative, and celebrate wins to support smooth implementation and ongoing sustainment.
